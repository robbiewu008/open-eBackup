About the OceanProtect

The OceanProtect helps easily configure and manage data protection services.

Operating System and Browser Compatibility

Before using the OceanProtect, check if the management terminal meets requirements.

The OceanProtect is compatible with various operating systems (OSs) and browsers. However, partially compatible browsers may fail to provide ease-of-use experience.

If the display effect of the OceanProtect is not user-friendly, you can adjust the zoom ratio of the browser to obtain the optimal display effect.
