Downloading Jobs

storage systemcloud full-stack backup service allows you to download existing jobs.

Procedure

  1. Choose Insight > Jobs.
  2. Download the list of running or historical jobs.

    1. Click the Execution Jobs or Historical Jobs tab to go to the job list.
    2. Optional: In the upper right corner of the job list, select the start time of the task to filter jobs. Alternatively, click Task Type > Object Type > Job Status > to filter jobs. Or you can click Object > Job ID > to search for jobs. Or you can click next to Start Time to sort jobs.
    3. Click Download above the job list to export the list.

      You can also click Download in a row to download the corresponding job.
