Manually Reclaiming Space

After all copies of a resource are deleted, the file system where the resource copies reside is also deleted. If the capacity of a deleted file system reaches a specified threshold, the system automatically reclaims the space of the file system. Otherwise, the automatic reclamation mechanism will not be triggered. As a result, the used capacity of the local storage may be inconsistent with the actual situation. In this case, you need to manually reclaim the space of deleted file systems.

Precautions

Service performance may be affected during the execution of space reclamation jobs.

Procedure

  1. Log in to DeviceManager.

    1. Choose System > Infrastructure > Cluster Management.
    2. In the Local Cluster Nodes area of the Backup Cluster tab page, click the node name.
    3. In the Node detail dialog box that is displayed, click Open the device management platform to go to DeviceManager.

  2. Choose System > Storage Pools.
  3. Click More on the right of the desired storage pool and choose Reclaim Space.

    Alternatively, click the name of the desired storage pool. In the upper right corner of the page that is displayed, click Operation and choose Reclaim Space.

  4. Confirm your operation as prompted.
