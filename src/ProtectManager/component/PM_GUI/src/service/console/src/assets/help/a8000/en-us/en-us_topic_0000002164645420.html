Step 1: Enabling the NFSv4.1 Service (Applicable to the OceanProtect E6000 Appliance)

Before performing backup operations, you must enable the NFSv4.1 service. Otherwise, the backup will fail.

Procedure

  1. Log in to DeviceManager. For details, see Logging In to DeviceManager.
  2. Choose Settings > Share Settings.
  3. In the Account drop-down list, select the vStore for which you want to enable the NFSv4.1 service.
  4. Click the NFS Service tab and click Modify.
  5. Select Enable next to NFSv4.1 Service.
  6. Click Save. A Danger dialog box is displayed.

    Confirm the information in the dialog box and select I have read and understand the consequences associated with performing this operation.

  7. Click OK.
