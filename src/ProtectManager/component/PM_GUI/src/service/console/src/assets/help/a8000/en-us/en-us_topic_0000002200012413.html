GBase 8a Database Resources Cannot Be Registered After the OceanProtect Is Upgraded

Symptom

The client is installed for the GBase8a cluster on the OceanProtect of the source version without registering resources. After the OceanProtect and client are upgraded, GBase8a database resources fail to be registered.

Possible Cause

After the upgrade, the environment runs so slowly that the resource registration takes a long time and the overall job running time exceeds the default limit (90 seconds). As a result, the resource registration times out and fails.

Procedure

  1. Log in to the background of all GBase 8a nodes.
  2. In the background of each GBase 8a node, run the following commands in sequence to restart the client service (wait 10 to 30 seconds after the client service is stopped). The following commands assume that the client is installed in /opt/.

    # Stopping the client service:
sh /opt/DataBackup/ProtectClient/stop.sh
    # Starting the client service:
sh /opt/DataBackup/ProtectClient/start.sh

  3. On the management page of the OceanProtect foreground, try to register the resources again.
