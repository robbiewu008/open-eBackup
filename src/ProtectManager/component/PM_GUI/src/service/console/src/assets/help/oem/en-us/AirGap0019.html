Viewing Storage Devices

Procedure

  1. Choose Data Security > Air Gap.

    For 1.5.0, choose Explore > Air Gap.

  2. Click the Storage Devices tab.
  3. In the storage device list, view the storage device information. Table 1 describes the related parameters.

    Table 1 Storage device parameters

    Parameter

    Description

    Name

    Storage device name.

    ESN

    ESN of the storage device.

    Device Status

    Storage device status. The value can be Online or Offline.

    Replication Link Status

    Replication link status. The status depends on the current time and whether the storage device is associated with an Air Gap policy.

    • Connected: An Air Gap policy has been associated and enabled, and the current time is in the replication time window of the Air Gap policy. Alternatively, an Air Gap policy has been associated and disabled.
    • Disconnected: An Air Gap policy has been associated and enabled, and the current time is not within the replication time window of the Air Gap policy.
    • Unknown: The storage device has been associated with an Air Gap policy and is offline.
      NOTE:

      If the storage device is not associated with an Air Gap policy, the replication link status is displayed as --.

    Associate Air Gap Policy

    Air Gap policy associated with the storage device. The name and interval of an Air Gap policy are displayed in the list.

    NOTE:

    If the storage device is not associated with an Air Gap policy, -- is displayed.

    Air Gap Policy Status

    Whether the Air Gap policy has been applied to the storage device.

    • Applying: The Air Gap policy has been associated and enabled.
    • Not Applied: The Air Gap policy has been associated and disabled.
      NOTE:

      If the storage device is not associated with an Air Gap policy, -- is displayed.
