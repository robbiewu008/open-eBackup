Modifying the Air Gap Policy Associated with a Storage Device

This section describes how to modify an Air Gap policy associated with a storage device. You can change the Air Gap policy, select another logical port, or choose to enable or disable the Forcibly Suspend Replication function for the storage device.

Procedure

  1. Choose Data Security > Air Gap.

    For 1.5.0, choose Explore > Air Gap.

  2. Click the Storage Devices tab.
  3. Locate the row that contains the storage device for which the Air Gap policy is to be modified, and choose More > Modify Association.
  4. In the window that is displayed on the right, set related information. Table 1 describes the parameters.
  5. Click OK.
