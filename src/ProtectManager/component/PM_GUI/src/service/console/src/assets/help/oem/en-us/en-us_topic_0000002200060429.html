Step 2: Creating a Periodic Archive Plan of Replication Copies

This section describes how to associate a resource with an SLA if the periodic archiving of replication copies generated by the resource is required.

Procedure

  1. Log in to the management page of the cluster where the replication copies reside.
  2. Choose Explore > Copy Data > Databases > Dameng.
  3. Locate the resource whose replication copies need to be archived and choose More > Protect.
  4. Select the created archive SLA.

    You can also click Create to create an SLA.

  5. Click OK.
