Modifying the Capacity Alarm Threshold of Archive Storage

The OceanProtect allows you to modify the capacity alarm threshold of archive storage. After the modification, the OceanProtect reports an alarm when the used capacity reaches the new threshold.

Prerequisites

Archive storage has been added.

Procedure

  1. Choose System > Infrastructure > Archive Storage Library.
  1. On the Object Storage tab page, click More on the right of the target archive storage and select Modify Archive Storage.
  2. Modify the capacity alarm threshold.

    Set the upper threshold of the used capacity of a bucket. When the used capacity reaches the threshold, an alarm is reported.

  3. Click OK.
