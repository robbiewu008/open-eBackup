Step 5: Configuring a Replication Service Port

On the OceanProtect WebUI at the local and remote ends, you can configure replication service ports for remote replication data transmission.

Prerequisites

The replication network IP addresses have been planned at the local and remote ends.

Precautions

Ensure that no job is running during network configuration.

Procedure

  1. Choose System > Infrastructure > Cluster Management.
  2. On the Backup Clusters tab page, click Modify Service Network.
  3. On the Replication Network tab page, select at least one port for replication services for each node.
  4. Click OK.
