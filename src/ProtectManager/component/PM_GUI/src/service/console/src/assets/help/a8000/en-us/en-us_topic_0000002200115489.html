Erasing Tapes

You can clear data from a tape by erasing the tape.

Prerequisites

  • A tape resides in the tape library.
  • A driver is available.

    When you erase a tape, the system automatically selects an available driver and loads the tape to the driver for erasing. Unload the tape from the driver after the tape is successfully erased.

Precautions

Tapes whose write status is Error cannot be erased.

Procedure

  1. Choose System > Infrastructure > Archive Storage Library.
  2. On the Tape tab page, select a node and click Tape Library.
  3. Click the Name of a tape library. In the window that is displayed, click the Tape tab.
  4. In the row where the tape to be erased resides, choose More > Erase.
  5. Read the warning message carefully, select I have read and understand the consequences associated with performing this operation., and click OK.
