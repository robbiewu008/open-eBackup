Stopping Jobs

You can stop eight types of jobs on the OceanCyber WebUI, including: resource scanning, ransomware detection snapshot generation, restoration to the original location, share path restoration, snapshot deletion, resource protection, protection modification, and intelligent detection.

Procedure

  1. Choose Jobs.
  2. Click the Current Jobs tab to display the job list.
  3. (Optional) In the upper right corner of the job list, pick a time point in Start Time to filter jobs. Alternatively, you can filter the jobs by clicking next to Job Type, Object Type, or Job Status. Alternatively, you can click next to Object or Job ID to search for jobs. Alternatively, you can click next to Start Time to sort jobs.
  4. Select one or more jobs and click Stop.
  5. In the dialog box that is displayed, click OK.
