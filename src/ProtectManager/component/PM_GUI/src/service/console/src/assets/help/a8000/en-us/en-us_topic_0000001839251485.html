Modifying an Air Gap Policy

This section describes how to modify an Air Gap policy.

Precautions

An Air Gap policy that has been associated with a storage device cannot be modified.

Procedure

  1. On the top menu bar, choose Explore > Air Gap.
  2. Click the Air Gap Policies tab.
  3. Locate the row that contains the item for which the Air Gap policy is to be modified, and choose More > Modify.
  4. In the window that is displayed on the right, set related information. Table 1 describes the parameters.
  5. Click OK.
