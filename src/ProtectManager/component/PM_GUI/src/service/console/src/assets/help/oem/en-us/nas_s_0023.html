Step 3: Configuring NAS Share Information (Applicable to OceanStor V5/OceanStor Pacific/NetApp ONTAP)

This section describes how to configure the FQDN/IP address and authentication information for a NAS share discovered through device registration.

Procedure

  1. Choose Protection > File Systems > NAS Shares.

    For 1.5.0, choose Protection > File Services > NAS Shares.

  2. Select the target NAS share and choose More > Modify to configure the FQDN/IP address of a NAS share.

    For details about parameters related to FQDN/IP address, see Table 1.

  3. If a NAS share has authentication information, configure the authentication information for the NAS share.

    For details about parameters related to NAS share authentication information, see Table 1.

  4. Click OK.
