Configuring the Capacity Alarm Threshold of the Local Storage

This section describes operations to modify the capacity alarm threshold of local storage on Storage System. After the modification, the system reports an alarm when the used capacity reaches the new threshold.

Procedure

  1. Choose System > Infrastructure > Local Storage.
  2. In the Available Capacity area of the Local Storage tab page, click on the right of Threshold.
  3. Modify the capacity alarm threshold of the local storage.

    Set the upper threshold of the used capacity of the local storage. When the used capacity reaches the threshold, an alarm is reported.

  4. Click OK.
