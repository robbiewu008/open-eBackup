Managing Alarms and Events

Procedure

Managing alarms

  1. Choose Alarms and Events > Alarms.
  2. (Optional) Click next to Severity or Object or next to Occurred At to filter alarms. Alternatively, click next to Alarm ID to search for alarms.
  3. Click the content in the Description column of an alarm and handle the alarm by referring to Suggestion displayed on the right.
  4. (Optional) Clear an alarm.

    1. In the alarm list, select the alarms that you want to clear and click Clear.
    2. In the dialog box that is displayed, click OK.

Managing events

  1. Choose Alarms and Events > Events.
  2. (Optional) Click next to Severity or Object or next to Occurred At to filter events. Alternatively, click next to Alarm ID to search for events.
  3. Click the content in the Description column of an event and handle the event by referring to Suggestion displayed on the right.
  4. (Optional) Select the current events to be exported and click to export them.
