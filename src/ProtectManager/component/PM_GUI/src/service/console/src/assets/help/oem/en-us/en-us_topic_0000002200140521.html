A Backup Job Is Suspended Due to Disk Domain Faults of the Production Storage (Applicable to 1.6.0 and Later Versions)

Symptom

During backup of a VMware VM, the backup job is suspended in the "Prepare a backup job" phase.

Possible Causes

The disk domain of the production storage where the VM resides is faulty.

Troubleshooting

  1. Use a browser to log in to the vCenter Server and check whether the jobs related to this VM, for example, snapshot creation, are suspended.

    • If yes, go to 2.
    • If no, contact technical support engineers.

  2. Log in to the storage device corresponding to the datastore where the VM resides, view alarms related to disk domains, and handle the alarms according to the alarm handling suggestions.
