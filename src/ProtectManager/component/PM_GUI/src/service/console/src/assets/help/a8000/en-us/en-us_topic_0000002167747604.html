Step 3: (Optional) Enabling Replication Link Encryption (Applicable When the OceanProtect Serves as Backup Storage)

You need to enable replication link encryption if required. Skip this section for the OceanProtect X3000 Appliance because it does not support replication link encryption.

Prerequisites

IPsec policies have been created for the replication network logical ports on the storage devices at both ends of the replication link. For details, see Step 2: (Optional) Creating an IPsec Policy (Applicable When the OceanProtect Serves as Backup Storage).

Procedure

  1. Choose System > Security > Data Security.
  2. In the Encryption Settings area, click Modify on the right of the page and enable Replication Link Encryption.
  3. Click Save.
