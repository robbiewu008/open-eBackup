How Do I Retry a Failed VMware Restoration Job? (Applicable to 1.6.0 and Later Versions)

This section describes how to retry a failed VMware restoration job.

Precautions

Only failed VMware restoration jobs generated in 1.6.0 and later versions can be retried. The VMware restoration jobs that failed before the system upgrade cannot be retried.

Procedure

  1. In the left navigation pane, choose Jobs.
  2. Switch to the Historical Jobs tab page, filter out the jobs for which Job Type is Restore, Object Type is VMware VM, and Job Status is Failed.
  3. In the row of the target job, click the job type and rectify the fault based on the suggestions under Events.
  4. After the fault is rectified, enter the handling suggestions.

    1. In the row of the target job, choose More > Handle.
    2. On the Handle page that is displayed on the right, enter handling suggestions.

      For handling suggestions, you are advised to enter the failure cause of the job and whether the fault is rectified.

    3. Click OK.

  5. In the row of the target job whose Handling Status is Handled, choose More > Retry, and confirm the operation as prompted. The system will deliver the restoration job again.

    • Alternatively, you can choose batch retry for desired jobs by performing the following operations: enter the handling suggestions for all jobs to be retried and click Batch Retry above the job list; on the displayed page, select the jobs to be retried and click OK; then confirm the operation as prompted. In this case, the system will re-deliver the restoration jobs in batches.
    • A job can be retried only once. If the job still fails after a retry, enter the handling suggestions for the re-delivered job and click Retry.
