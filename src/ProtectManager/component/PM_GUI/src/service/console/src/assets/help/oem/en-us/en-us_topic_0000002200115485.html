Viewing the Job Progress

This section describes how to view data protection job information so that you can know about the running status and details of data protection jobs.

Procedure

  1. Choose Jobs.

    For 1.5.0, choose .

  2. View Current Jobs or Historical Jobs.

    1. Click the Current Jobs or Historical Jobs tab to go to the job list.
    2. Optional: In the upper right corner of the job list, select the start time of the job and SLA policy name to filter jobs. Alternatively, click next to Job Type, Object Type, or Job Status to filter jobs, click next to Object or Job ID to search for jobs, or click next to Start Time to sort jobs.
    3. Click Job Type to expand the job details and view the protected object information, job execution details, as well as the SLA policy configuration and advanced protection settings of the backup job.
    • For a backup job, deduplication and compression are automatically performed by the product after data backup. In the Basic Info area, Data Volume Before Reduction indicates the data volume of backup copies before deduplication and compression.

      If expired copies are deleted at the same time in the background during backup, Data Volume Before Reduction will be inaccurate.

      Data Volume Before Reduction is the difference between the actual data volume before reduction and the data volume of deleted copies before reduction. (If the difference is negative, there is no data before reduction.)

    • By default, copy expiration jobs are not displayed in the job list. To view copy expiration jobs, select Show copy expiration jobs in the upper right corner of the job list.
    • Retention period of historical jobs: A maximum of 200,000 historical jobs can be retained for a maximum of three months. The excess historical jobs will be moved to the deletion table. A maximum of 400,000 historical jobs can be retained in the deletion table for a maximum of six months.
    • The job priorities in descending order are as follows: restoration and restoration drill > live mount > host registration and client update > backup, archive, and replication > copy deletion.
