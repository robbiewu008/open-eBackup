Using Real-Time Ransomware Detection (Applicable to Block Services)

Ransomware attacks have similar I/O behavior characteristics. By analyzing LUN I/O behavior characteristics, the system quickly identifies abnormal LUNs and performs deep content analysis on the abnormal LUNs to detect LUNs infected by ransomware. Then, secure snapshots are created for the LUNs, and alarms are reported to notify the data protection administrator, limiting the impact of ransomware and reducing losses.
