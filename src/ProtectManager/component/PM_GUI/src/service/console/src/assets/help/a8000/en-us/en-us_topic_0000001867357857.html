Managing Drill Plans

You can modify, activate, disable, and delete drill plans.

On the top navigation bar, choose Explore > Restoration Drills. On the Overview page, perform management operations by referring to Table 1.

Table 1 Drill plan management operations

Operation

Operation Guide

Navigation Path

Viewing a drill plan

Scenario

This operation enables you to learn about the drill type, running status, execution period, next drill time, and latest job result of a drill plan.

In the drill plan list, click or to view the drill plan in list or card mode.

Modifying a drill plan

Scenario

This operation enables you to modify a drill plan.

Select a drill plan and choose More > Modify.

Activating a drill plan

Scenario

This operation enables you to activate a drill plan.

Notes

  • One-off drill plans cannot be activated.
  • Drill plans that are being executed cannot be activated.

Select a drill plan and choose More > Activate.

Disabling a drill plan

Scenario

This operation enables you to disable a drill plan.

Notes

  • One-off drill plans cannot be disabled.
  • Drill plans that are being executed cannot be disabled.

Select a drill plan and choose More > Disable.

Deleting a drill plan

Scenario

This operation enables you to delete an unnecessary drill plan.

Notes

Drill plans that are being executed cannot be deleted.

Select a drill plan and choose More > Delete.
