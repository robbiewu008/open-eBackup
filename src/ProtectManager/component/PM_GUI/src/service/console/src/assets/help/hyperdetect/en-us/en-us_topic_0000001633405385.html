Managing Alarms and Events

Procedure

Managing alarms:

  1. Choose Insight > Alarms and Events > Alarm.
  2. (Optional) Click next to Severity or Object, or click next to Occurred At to filter alarms. Alternatively, click next to Alarm ID to search for alarms.
  3. Click Description of an alarm and handle the alarm by referring to Suggestion displayed on the right.
  4. (Optional) Clear alarms.

    1. Select the alarm to be cleared and click Clear.
    2. In the dialog box that is displayed, click OK.

Managing events:

  1. Choose Insight > Alarms and Events > Events.
  2. (Optional) Click next to Severity or Object, or click next to Occurred At to filter events. Alternatively, click next to Alarm ID to search for events.
  3. Click Description of an event and handle the event by referring to Suggestion displayed on the right.
  4. (Optional) Select events to be cleared and click to export these events.
