Managing Air Gap Policies

OceanCyber supports the modification or Air Gap policy.

You need to log in to the OceanCyber, choose Air Gap, and find the Air Gap policy to be operated. Table 1 describes the related operations.

Table 1 Operations related to Air Gap policies

Job

Description

Navigation Path

Modifying an Air Gap policy

Scenario

This operation allows you to modify an Air Gap policy.

Note

An Air Gap policy that has been associated with a storage device cannot be modified.

In the row that contains the Air Gap policy, choose More > Modify.

Deleting an Air Gap policy

Scenario

This operation enables you to delete an Air Gap policy that is no longer needed.

Note

Air Gap policies that have been associated with storage devices cannot be deleted.

In the row that contains the air gap policy, choose More > Delete.
