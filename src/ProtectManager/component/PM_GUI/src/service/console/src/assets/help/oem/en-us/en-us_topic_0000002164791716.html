Managing Backup Storage Devices (Applicable to 1.6.0 and Later Versions)

Multiple storage pools are supported as of 1.6.0. In 1.6.0, the "backup storage unit" concept is renamed as "backup storage device", and the "backup storage unit" concept is added to represent a storage pool in the backup storage device.
