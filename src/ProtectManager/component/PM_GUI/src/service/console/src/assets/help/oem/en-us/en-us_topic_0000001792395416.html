Step 2: Authorizing a Resource

After registering Kubernetes with the management platform, you can authorize Kubernetes resources to a specified data protection administrator. Subsequently, only the system administrator and the data protection administrator can perform data protection operations.

Procedure

  1. Choose Protection > Containers > Kubernetes FlexVolume.
  2. Choose More > Authorize Resource on the right area of a displayed resource.
  3. Select the data protection administrator to be authorized.
  4. Click OK.

    After the authorization is successful, on the Cluster page, the owner of the resource is the data protection administrator(s).
