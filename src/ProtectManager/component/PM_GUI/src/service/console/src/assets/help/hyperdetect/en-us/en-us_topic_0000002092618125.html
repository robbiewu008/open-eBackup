Viewing the Job Progress

Procedure

  1. Choose Jobs.
  2. View the progress of current jobs or historical jobs.

    1. Click the Current Jobs or Historical Jobs tab. The job list is displayed.
    2. (Optional) In the upper right corner of the job list, pick a time point in Start Time to filter jobs. Or click next to Job Type, Object Type, and Job Status to filter jobs. Or click next to Object or Job ID to search for jobs. Or click next to Start Time to sort jobs.
    3. Click an item in the Job Type column to expand job details and view the protected object information and job execution details.
