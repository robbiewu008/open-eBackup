Disabling the Air Gap Policy Associated with a Storage Device

This section describes how to disable the Air Gap policy associated with a storage device. After this function is disabled, replication jobs can be executed based on the replication time set in the replication SLA policy and are not affected by the Air Gap replication time window.

Procedure

  1. On the top menu bar, choose Data Security > Air Gap.
  2. Click the Storage Device tab.
  3. In the row that contains the storage device for which the Air Gap policy is to be disabled, choose More > Disable Policy.
  4. In the dialog box that is displayed, confirm the information and click OK.
