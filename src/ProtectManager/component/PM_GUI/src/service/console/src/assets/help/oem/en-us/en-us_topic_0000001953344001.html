Obtaining the AK and SK at the Production End

AK is the access certificate used for accessing object storage, and SK is the security certificate used for accessing object storage. The environment at the production end can be OceanStor Pacific, Huawei Cloud, or Alibaba private cloud.

Obtaining the AK and SK on OceanStor Pacific or Alibaba private cloud

The methods of obtaining the AK and SK are the same when OceanStor Pacific and Alibaba private cloud are deployed. The following part uses OceanStor Pacific deployed at the production end as an example.

  1. Log in to the OceanStor Pacific management page.
  2. Choose Resources > Access > Account.
  3. Click Create, configure basic account information, and click OK.

    After the account is created, the AK and SK are displayed.

    After an account is created on OceanStor Pacific, the AK and SK are displayed. After that, the SK cannot be obtained. Keep the AK and SK secure.

    After an account is created on the Alibaba private cloud, you can view the account information to obtain the AK and SK.

Obtaining the AK and SK on Huawei Cloud

  1. Log in to the Huawei Cloud management page.
  2. Click the account name in the upper right corner of the page.

  3. Choose My Space > My Settings > Access Keys and click Add Access Key.
  4. The AK is automatically generated on Huawei Cloud. The SK is automatically downloaded to the local end.

    You can download the AK and SK from Huawei Cloud only once. Keep the AK and SK properly.
