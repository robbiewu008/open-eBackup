Managing a Mount Update Policy of a NAS File System

This section describes the operations that you can perform on mount update policies on the management page.

You need to log in to the management page and choose Explore > Data Utilization Policies > Mount Update Policies.

Table 1 describes the related operations.

Table 1 Operations related to live mount

Operation

Description

Navigation Path

Cloning a mount update policy

Scenario

Cloning a mount update policy helps you quickly create an update policy based on an existing mount update policy.

In the row of the target mount update policy, choose More > Clone.

Modifying a mount update policy

Scenario

This operation enables you to modify a mount update policy.

In the row of the target mount update policy, choose More > Modify.

Deleting a mount update policy

Scenario

This operation enables you to delete a mount update policy that is not required anymore.

Note

A mount update policy that has been associated with a mount job cannot be deleted.

In the row of the target mount update policy, choose More > Delete.
