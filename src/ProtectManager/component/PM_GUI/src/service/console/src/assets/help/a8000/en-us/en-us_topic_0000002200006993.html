Backup and Restoration Jobs Fail Due to FusionCompute Node Faults

Symptom

During backup or restoration of FusionCompute VMs, the VRM management node becomes faulty, triggering an active/standby switchover. As a result, the backup or restoration job fails.

Possible Cause

When two FusionCompute management nodes are deployed in active/standby mode, it takes 1 to 2 minutes for the standby node to take over services if a fault occurs, such as hardware or key process suspension, OS breakdown, or network interruption. (The takeover may take 2 to 3 minutes, depending on the actual load in the environment.) During the takeover process, the management service is abnormal, and interfaces cannot be invoked.

Troubleshooting

  1. Check whether the FusionCompute management node is restored.

    Use https://IP address of the FusionCompute management node:8443 to log in to the FusionCompute WebUI. If the login is successful, the FusionCompute management node has been restored.

  2. After the FusionCompute management node is restored, perform the backup or restoration job again.
