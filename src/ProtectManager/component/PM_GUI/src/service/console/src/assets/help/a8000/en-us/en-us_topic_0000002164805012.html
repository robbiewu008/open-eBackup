Backing Up a NAS Share

This section describes how to complete backup configuration. For 1.6.0 and later versions, IntelliMate (smart assistant) provides step-by-step help to streamline user operations. Specifically, log in to the OceanProtect WebUI and click in the upper part of the page. On the page that is displayed, click Learn How to Perform Backup Operations, select the type of the resource to be protected, and click Next to complete the backup configuration according to the backup configuration guide.

If the storage device where a NAS share to be protected resides is not OceanStor V5, OceanStor Pacific, or NetApp ONTAP, or only one NAS share needs to be protected, you can manually register the NAS share with the OceanProtect by following instructions in (Optional) Step 5: Registering a NAS Share.

  • Step 1: (Optional) Obtaining the CA Certificate of a Storage Device (Applicable to OceanStor V5/OceanStor Pacific/NetApp ONTAP)
  • Step 2: (Optional) Adding a Storage Device (Applicable to OceanStor V5/OceanStor Pacific/NetApp ONTAP)
  • (Optional) Step 3: Configuring NAS Share Information (Applicable to OceanStor V5/OceanStor Pacific/NetApp ONTAP)
  • Step 4: Configuring Access Permissions (Applicable to OceanStor V5/OceanStor Pacific/NetApp ONTAP)
  • (Optional) Step 5: Registering a NAS Share
  • (Optional) Step 6: Creating a Rate Limiting Policy
  • Step 7: Creating a Backup SLA
  • Step 8: Enabling the NFSv4.1 Service (Applicable to OceanProtect X Series Backup Appliances and OceanProtect E1000 (with the OceanProtect Used as Backup Storage))
  • Step 8: Enabling the NFSv4.1 Service (Applicable to the OceanProtect E6000 Appliance)
  • Step 9: Performing Backup
