Deleting Modifying Operation Restrictions on Infected Copies

This section describes how to delete the operation restrictions on infected copies.

Prerequisites

Operation restrictions on infected copies have been configured.

Procedure

  1. Choose Data Security > Ransomware Protection > Ransomware Protection and WORM Configurations.
  2. Click Operation Restrictions on Infected Copies.
  3. On the Operation Restrictions on Infected Copies tab page, select one or more target resource names and click Delete Restriction.

    You can also choose More > Delete Restriction on the right of the row of the target resource.

  4. In the following dialog box that is displayed, confirm the information, select I have read and understand the consequences associated with performing this operation., and click OK.
