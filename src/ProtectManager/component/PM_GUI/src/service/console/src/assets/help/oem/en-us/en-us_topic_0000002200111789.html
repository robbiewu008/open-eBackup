Constraints

  • Backup and restoration jobs cannot be executed for a resource at the same time.
  • Live mount and instant recovery are not supported.
  • If an expiration or deletion job is triggered for a copy, the copy cannot be used for replication and restoration jobs.
  • Nutanix VMs cannot be registered using the Prism Central (PC) platform. They can be registered only using the Prism Element (PE) platform.
  • For the OceanProtect X3000/X6000 Appliance, copies with more than 20 million files cannot be indexed. For the OceanProtect X8000 Appliance, copies with more than 50 million files cannot be indexed.
