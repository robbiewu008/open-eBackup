Removing the Air Gap Policy Associated with a Storage Device

This section describes how to remove the Air Gap policy associated with a storage device if the Air Gap policy is no longer used.

Procedure

  1. On the top menu bar, choose Data Security > Air Gap.
  2. Click the Storage Device tab.
  3. In the row that contains the storage device for which the Air Gap policy is to be removed, choose More > Remove Policy.
  4. In the dialog box that is displayed, confirm the information and click OK.
