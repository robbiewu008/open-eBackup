Managing Alarms and Events

Procedure

Managing alarms:

  1. Choose Alarms and Events > Alarms.

    For 1.5.0, choose Insight > Alarms and Events > Alarms.

  2. (Optional) Click next to Severity or Object, or click next to Occurred At to filter alarms. You can also search for alarms by node name.
  3. Click Description of an alarm and handle the alarm by referring to Suggestion displayed on the right.
  4. (Optional) Clear alarms.

    1. Select the alarms to be cleared and click Clear.
    2. In the dialog box that is displayed, click OK.

Managing events:

  1. Choose Alarms and Events > Events.

    For 1.5.0, choose Insight > Alarms and Events > Events.

  2. (Optional) Click next to Severity or Object, or click next to Occurred At to filter events. Alternatively, click next to Nodes to search for events.
  3. Click Description of an event and handle the event by referring to Suggestion displayed on the right.
  4. (Optional) Select the current events to be exported and click to export them.
