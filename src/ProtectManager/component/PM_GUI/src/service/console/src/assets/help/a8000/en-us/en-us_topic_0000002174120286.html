What Are the Transmission Modes for Backup and Restoration of FusionCompute?

When the OceanProtect provides protection for the FusionCompute virtualization environment, the data transmission modes for backup and restoration vary depending on the production storage of FusionCompute.

LAN-Free Mode

If the production storage of FusionCompute is Huawei scale-out block storage or eVol storage, the OceanProtect queries the mapping information of the target disk on Huawei scale-out storage or eVol storage and mounts the target volume to the host where the client is installed. In this way, the data transmission rate is not limited by the bandwidth of the FusionCompute storage LAN, achieving higher backup and restoration efficiency.

Figure 1 Backup and restoration in LAN-free mode

LAN-based Mode

If the production storage of FusionCompute is virtualized storage, such as virtualized local disks, virtualized SAN storage (IP SAN and FC SAN), and virtualized NAS storage, FusionCompute obtains disk location and other information from VRM through the host where the client is installed and sets up a connection with the CNA node to transmit backup and restoration data.
