Downloading Jobs

This section describes how to download jobs from the OceanCyber WebUI.

Procedure

  1. Choose Jobs.
  2. Download the list of current or historical jobs.

    1. Click the Current Jobs or Historical Jobs tab. The job list is displayed.
    2. (Optional) In the upper right corner of the job list, pick a time point in Start Time to filter jobs. Alternatively, you can filter the jobs by clicking next to Job Type, Object Type, or Job Status. Alternatively, you can click next to Object or Job ID to search for jobs. Alternatively, you can click next to Start Time to sort jobs.
    3. Click Download All above the job list to export the list.

      You can also click More > Download in the row of a job to download the job.
