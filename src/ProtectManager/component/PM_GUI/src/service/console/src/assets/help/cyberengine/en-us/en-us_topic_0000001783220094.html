Modifying the Air Gap Policy Associated with a Storage Device

This section describes how to modify an Air Gap policy associated with a storage device. You can change another Air Gap policy for the storage device or change ports that support replication within the time window set in the Air Gap policy.

Procedure

  1. On the top menu bar, choose Data Security > Air Gap.
  2. Click the Storage Device tab.
  3. In the row that contains the storage device for which the Air Gap policy is to be modified, choose More > Modify Association.
  4. In the window that is displayed on the right, set related information. Table 1 describes the parameters.
  5. Click OK.
