Viewing the Job Progress

Procedure

  1. Choose .
  2. View the progress of Current Jobs or Historical Jobs.

    1. Click the Current Jobs or Historical Jobs tab.
    2. Optional: In the upper right corner of the job list, select the start time of the job and SLA policy name to filter jobs. Alternatively, click next to Job Type, Object Type, or Job Status to filter jobs. Or you can click next to Object or Job ID to search for jobs. Or you can click next to Start Time to sort jobs.
    3. Click Job Type to expand the job details and view the protected object information, job execution details, as well as the SLA policy configuration and advanced protection settings of the backup job.
