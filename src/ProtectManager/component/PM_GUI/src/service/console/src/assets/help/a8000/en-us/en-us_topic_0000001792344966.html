(Optional) Step 3: Enabling Replication Link Encryption

You need to enable replication link encryption if required. Skip this section for OceanProtect X3000 because it does not support replication link encryption.

Procedure

  1. Choose System > Security > Data Security.
  2. In the Encryption Settings area, click Modify on the right of the page and enable Replication Link Encryption.
  3. Click Save.
