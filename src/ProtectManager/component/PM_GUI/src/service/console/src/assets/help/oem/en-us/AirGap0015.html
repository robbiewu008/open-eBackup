Viewing Air Gap Policies

Procedure

  1. Choose Data Security > Air Gap.

    For 1.5.0, choose Explore > Air Gap.

  2. Click the Air Gap Policies tab.
  3. In the Air Gap policy list, view the Air Gap policy information. Table 1 describes the related parameters.

    Table 1 Air Gap policy parameters

    Parameter

    Description

    Name

    Name of an Air Gap policy.

    Interval

    Interval of executing Air Gap policies.

    Replication Time Window

    Replication time window allowed by the Air Gap policy.

    Number of Associated Storage Devices

    Number of storage devices associated with Air Gap policies.

    Description

    Description of an Air Gap policy.
