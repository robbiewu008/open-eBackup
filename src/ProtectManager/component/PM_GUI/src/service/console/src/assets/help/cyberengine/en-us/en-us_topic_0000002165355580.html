Modifying Expiration Warning Days

This section describes how to modify the expiration warning days of a certificate on the OceanCyber WebUI.

Procedure

  1. Choose System > Security > Certificates.
  2. Click More on the right of the desired certificate and choose Modify Alarm Threshold.
  3. Set the alarm threshold to an integer ranging from 7 to 180. The system reports an alarm when the expiration time of a certificate reaches the value of this parameter.
  4. Click OK.
