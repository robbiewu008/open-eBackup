Activating or Disabling the SAML SSO Configuration

After the SAML SSO configuration is created, it is activated by default. This section describes how to disable the SAML SSO configuration. After the SAML SSO configuration is disabled, the SAML SSO configuration is in the Inactivated status and the OceanProtect cannot be accessed through eDME. You can also perform the operations in this section to reactivate the SAML SSO configuration.

Procedure

  1. Choose System > Security > SAML SSO Configuration.
  2. Locate the row that contains the target SAML SSO configuration, and click Activate or Disable.
