Enabling the Air Gap Policy Associated with a Storage Device

This section describes how to enable the Air Gap policy associated with a storage device. After this function is enabled, replication jobs can be executed only in the replication time window allowed by the policy.

Procedure

  1. On the top menu bar, choose Data Security > Air Gap.
  2. Click the Storage Device tab.
  3. In the row that contains the storage device for which the Air Gap policy is to be enabled, choose More > Enable Policy.
  4. In the dialog box that is displayed, confirm the information and click OK.
