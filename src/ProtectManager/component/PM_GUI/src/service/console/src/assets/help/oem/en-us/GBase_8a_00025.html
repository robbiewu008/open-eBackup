Step 3: (Optional) Enabling Replication Link Encryption

You need to enable replication link encryption if required. Some models of this product do not support replication link encryption. For details, see the product documentation released on the technical support website or consult the product provider.

Prerequisites

IPsec policies have been created for the replication network logical ports on the storage devices at both ends of the replication link. For details, see (Optional) Step 2: Creating an IPsec Policy.

Procedure

  1. Choose System > Security > Data Security.
  2. In the Encryption Settings area, click Modify on the right of the page and enable Replication Link Encryption.
  3. Click Save.
