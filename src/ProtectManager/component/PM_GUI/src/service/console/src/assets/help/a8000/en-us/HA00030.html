Modifying HA Parameters

This section describes how to modify HA parameters.

Prerequisites

HA members have been added to the system.

Procedure

  1. Choose System > Infrastructure > Cluster Management.
  2. Click Backup Cluster.
  3. Choose HA Management > Modify HA Parameter.
  4. Modify Floating IP Address or Quorum Gateway.
  5. Click OK. After you modify the HA parameters, the status of the standby node is Setting. After a while, the system automatically refreshes the status and the status changes to Online.
