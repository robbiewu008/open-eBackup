Sender Settings

Through the alarm notification function, the system notifies users of alarm details by email.

Prerequisites

You have set Sender Email Address in Mail Service.

Procedure

  1. Choose System > Settings > Alarm Notification.
  2. Enable Sender Settings, click Modify, and modify the severity levels and types of alarms to be reported.
  3. Click Add to add email addresses of recipients.
  4. (Optional) Click Test to verify the connectivity between the OceanCyber 300 Data Security Appliance and the SMTP server.
