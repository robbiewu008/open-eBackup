Disabling the Air Gap Policy Associated with a Storage Device

This section describes how to disable the Air Gap policy associated with a storage device. After the policy is disabled, replication jobs can be executed based on the replication time set in the replication SLA policy and are not subject to the Air Gap replication time window.

Procedure

  1. Choose Data Security > Air Gap.

    For 1.5.0, choose Explore > Air Gap.

  2. Click the Storage Devices tab.
  3. Locate the row that contains the storage device for which the Air Gap policy is to be disabled, and choose More > Disable Policy.
  4. In the dialog box that is displayed, confirm the information and click OK.
