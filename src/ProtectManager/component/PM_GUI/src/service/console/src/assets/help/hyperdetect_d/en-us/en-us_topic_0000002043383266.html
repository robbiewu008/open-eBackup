Viewing Real-Time Ransomware Detection Results

This section describes how to view real-time ransomware detection results.

Procedure

  1. Choose Explore > Ransomware Protection > Overview.
  2. In the Real-time Ransomware Detection Alarms area, view alarms. If an alarm exists, perform the following steps to view the alarm information.

    1. Click the alarm information to go to the Alarms and Events page.
    2. Locate the alarm and click the alarm description. The LUN infected by ransomware is displayed.

      After the LUN infected by ransomware is handled, the alarm will not be automatically cleared. Manually clear the alarm on ProtectManager. After that, the alarm on DeviceManager will be automatically cleared. If you directly clear the alarm on DeviceManager, there will be a delay in automatic alarm clearance on the Overview page on ProtectManager.
