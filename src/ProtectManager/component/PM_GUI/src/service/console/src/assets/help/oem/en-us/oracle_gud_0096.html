What Should I Do If the Progress of an Oracle Data Protection Job Is Not Updated for More Than 10 Minutes?

Symptom

The progress of an Oracle data protection job is not updated for more than 10 minutes.

Troubleshooting

  1. Choose Jobs.
  2. On the Current Jobs tab page, locate the row of the job to be processed and choose More > Stop to stop the job. Then perform the job again or other jobs.
