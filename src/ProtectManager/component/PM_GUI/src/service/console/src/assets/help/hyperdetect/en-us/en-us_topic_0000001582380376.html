Viewing Job Progress

Procedure

  1. Choose Insight > Jobs.
  2. View the progress of Current Jobs or Historical Jobs.

    1. Click the Current Jobs or Historical Jobs tab.
    2. Optional: In the upper right corner of the job list, select the start time of the task to filter jobs. Alternatively, click Task Type > Object Type > Job Status > to filter jobs. Or you can click Object > Job ID > to search for jobs. Or you can click next to Start Time to sort jobs.
    3. Click Task Type, expand job details, and view the protected object information and job execution details.
