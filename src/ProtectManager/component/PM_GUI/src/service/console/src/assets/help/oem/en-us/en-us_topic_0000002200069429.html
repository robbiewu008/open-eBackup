What Should I Do If "Operation failed" Is displayed During Copy Data Viewing on the OceanProtect WebUI

Symptom

On the OceanProtect WebUI, error message "Operation failed" is displayed when you view copy data on the timeline.

Possible Cause

During log backup configuration, the configured log backup interval is too short. As a result, too many log copies are generated during the period specified in the log backup policy, and the product cannot correctly calculate the start time and end time of logs.

Procedure

Reconfigure the log backup interval by referring to Enable and configure the log backup function.
