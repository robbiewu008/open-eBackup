Managing Live Mount

On the OceanProtect WebUI, you can perform management operations related to live mount of volume copies.

You need to log in to the OceanProtect WebUI and choose Explore > Live Mount > Volume.

Table 1 describes the detailed operations.

Table 1 Operations related to live mount

Job

Description

Navigation Path

Modifying mount information

Scenario

If parameters such as the bandwidth, IOPS, and mount update policy of a mount copy are not configured when you create a live mount job, you can perform this operation to configure the parameters or modify the configured parameters.

Choose More > Modify in the row where the desired live mount information is located.

Destroying a mount

Scenario

This operation enables you to unmount a copy from a host.

Note

During destruction, you need to determine whether to retain the copy generated by live mount. If you choose to retain the copy, it will be retained based on the retention period set in Creating a Mount Update Policy or permanently retained (in the case when no mount update policy is set). Otherwise, the mounted copy is directly deleted.

Choose More > Destroy in the row where the desired live mount information is located.

Updating a mount copy

Scenario

This operation enables you to update a mount copy for a host instantly.

Note

A mount copy can be updated only when the live mount status is Available or Mounting failed.
  1. Choose More > Update in the row where the desired live mount information is located.
  2. Select Update to latest copy data or Update to specified copy data.

Disabling mount update

Scenario

Perform this operation if you do not need to update a mount copy based on the mount update policy or you want to prevent a mount copy from being manually updated or live mount parameters from being modified.

Choose More > Disable in the row where the desired live mount information is located.

Activating mount update

Scenario

After live mount is disabled, it can be reactivated. After the activation, mount copies are updated based on the mount update policy.

Choose More > Activate in the row where the desired live mount information is located.

Viewing mount details

Scenario

This operation enables you to view the details of a live mount.

Choose More > View in the row where the desired live mount information is located.

View details about the resource, copy data, and mount update policy.
