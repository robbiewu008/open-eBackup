Step 5: Authorizing a Resource

After registering the Kubernetes cluster with the management platform, you can authorize Kubernetes CSI resources to a specified data protection administrator. Subsequently, only the system administrator and the data protection administrator can perform data protection operations.

Procedure

  1. Choose Protection > Containers > Kubernetes CSI.
  2. Choose More > Authorize Resource on the right area of a displayed resource.
  3. Select one or more data protection administrators.
  4. Click OK.

    After the authorization is successful, on the Cluster page, the owner of the resource is the data protection administrator(s).
