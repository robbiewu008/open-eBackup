Constraints

The constraints on copy replication are as follows:

  • Replication copies cannot be used for restoration to any point in time.
  • Replication copies cannot be used for restoration to the original location.
  • Reverse replication copies cannot be replicated or archived.
  • Replication copies do not support global search.
  • Cascaded replication supports a maximum of three levels.
  • Replication is not supported between 1.3.0 or a later version and 1.2.1.
  • WORM properties of replication copies at the primary and secondary ends are the same.
  • Replication job rate limiting takes effect for the entire file system where the copies to be replicated reside.
  • Intra-domain replication copies do not support reverse replication or cascaded replication.
  • Log backup copies do not support replication.
