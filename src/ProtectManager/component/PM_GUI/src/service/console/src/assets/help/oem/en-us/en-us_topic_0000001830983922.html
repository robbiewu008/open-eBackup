Downloading Jobs

product allows you to download existing jobs.

Procedure

  1. Choose Insight > Jobs.
  2. Download the list of running or historical jobs.

    1. Click the Current Jobs or Historical Jobs tab to go to the job list.
    2. Optional: In the upper right corner of the job list, select the start time of the job and SLA policy name to filter jobs. Alternatively, click next to Job Type, Object Type, or Job Status to filter jobs. Or you can click next to Object or Job ID to search for jobs. Or you can click next to Start Time to sort jobs.
    3. Click Download above the job list to export the list.
      • You can also click Download in a row to download the corresponding job.
      • A maximum of 50,000 jobs can be downloaded at a time.
