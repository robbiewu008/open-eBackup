Disconnecting the Replication Link of a Storage Device (Applicable to 1.6.0 and Later Versions)

This section describes how to disconnect the replication link of a storage device. This operation will forcibly disconnect the replication link, causing the original Air Gap policy to be invalid. In this case, replication jobs cannot be performed on the device.

Prerequisites

The storage device has been associated with an Air Gap policy.

Procedure

  1. Choose Data Security > Air Gap.
  2. Click the Storage Devices tab.
  3. In the row of the target storage device, choose More > Disconnect Link.
  4. In the displayed dialog box shown as follows, confirm the information, select I have read and understand the consequences associated with performing this operation., and click OK.
