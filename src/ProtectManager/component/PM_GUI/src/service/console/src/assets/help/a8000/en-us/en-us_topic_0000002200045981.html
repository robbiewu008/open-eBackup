Constraints

The constraints on live mount are as follows:

  • Archive copies do not support live mount.
  • Imported copies do not support live mount.
  • For the OceanProtect E6000 Appliance (with OceanStor Pacific used as the backup storage) scenario, replication copies cannot be used for live mount.
