Managing Rate Limiting Policies

You can perform operations on rate limiting policies on ProtectManager.

Before performing these operations, you need to log in to ProtectManager and choose Protect > Protection Policies > Rate Limiting Policies. The rate limiting policy page is displayed.

Table 1 describes the relevant operations.

Table 1 Operations related to rate limiting policies

Operation

Description

Navigation Path

Modifying a rate limiting policy

Scenario

This operation enables you to modify a rate limiting policy.

In the row where the rate limiting policy resides, choose More > Modify.

Deleting a rate limiting policy

Scenario

This operation enables you to delete a rate limiting policy that is no longer used.

In the row where the rate limiting policy resides, choose More > Delete.
