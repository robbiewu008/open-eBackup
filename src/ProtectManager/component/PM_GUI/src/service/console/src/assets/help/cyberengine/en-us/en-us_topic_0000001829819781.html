Deleting a Report

Procedure

  1. On the top menu bar, choose Data Security > Report.
  2. To delete a single report, click in the lower right corner of the report. To delete multiple reports in batches, select the security reports to be deleted and click Delete.
  3. In the dialog box that is displayed, confirm the information and click OK.
