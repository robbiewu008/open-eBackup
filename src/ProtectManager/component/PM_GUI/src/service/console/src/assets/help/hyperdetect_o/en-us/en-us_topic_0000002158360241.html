Stopping Jobs

Procedure

  1. Choose Jobs.
  2. Click the Current Jobs tab. The job list is displayed.
  3. Optional: In the upper right corner of the job list, select the start time of the job and SLA policy name to filter jobs. Alternatively, click next to Job Type, Object Type, or Job Status to filter jobs, click next to Object or Job ID to search for jobs, or click next to Start Time to sort jobs.
  4. Select one or more jobs and click Stop.
  5. In the dialog box that is displayed, click OK.

    After you stop a job, if the job status changes to Stopped but the job details show that the job is successfully executed, the job has been successfully executed. The possible cause is that the copy has already been generated when you stop the job.
