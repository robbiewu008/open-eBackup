Viewing Job Progress

This section describes how to view job information so that you can know about the execution state and details of data jobs.

Procedure

  1. Choose Jobs.
  2. View the progress of Current Jobs or Historical Jobs.

    1. Click the Execution Jobs or Historical Jobs tab. The job list is displayed.
    2. (Optional) In the upper right corner of the job list, pick a time point in Start Time to filter jobs. Alternatively, you can filter the jobs by clicking next to Job Type, Object Type, or Job Status. Alternatively, you can click next to Object or Job ID to search for jobs. Alternatively, you can click next to Start Time to sort jobs.
    3. Click Job Type to expand job details and view the protected object information and job execution details.
