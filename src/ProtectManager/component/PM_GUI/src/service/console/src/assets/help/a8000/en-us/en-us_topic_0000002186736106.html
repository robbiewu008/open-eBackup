Instance Status Is Normal After a Restoration Job Is Executed, but Restoration Fails

Symptom

After a restoration job is executed, the instance status is normal, but the restoration fails.

Possible Causes

After the restoration job is executed, the instance is not released on the GaussDB management platform (TPOPS).

Procedure

  1. Log in to the TPOPS management page.

    https://TPOPS management plane IP address:8002/gaussdb, for example, https://192.168.10.2:8002/gaussdb

  2. Check the instance status.

    • If the status is Restore failed, go to 3 to 6.
    • If the status is Abnormal, go to 7.

  3. Choose Task Center.
  4. Select the failed job and click Task Details.
  5. Click Release Lock.
  6. Execute the restoration job by referring to Restoring GaussDB Instances.
  7. Reinstall the instance by referring to "GaussDB Installation" in the GaussDB Product Documentation of the corresponding version.
