Step 2: Checking the FusionCompute Interface Access Isolation Policy

If Man-Machine and Machine-Machine Interface Access Isolation Policy is displayed in System Management > Rights Management > Rights Management Policy on the FusionCompute management page, perform the operations in this section to change the current rule of Man-Machine and Machine-Machine Interface Access Isolation Policy to No. This ensures that the FusionCompute interface interconnection user can access FusionCompute through port 7443. Otherwise, skip this section.

Precautions

The navigation path varies depending on the FusionCompute version.

Procedure

The following operations use FusionCompute 8.6.0 as an example.

  1. Log in to FusionCompute using a browser.

    Login address: https://Floating IP address of the VRM node of FusionCompute:8443

  2. Choose System Management > Rights Management > Rights Management Policy.
  3. In the Other Strategies area, click Modify.
  4. Change the value of Man-Machine and Machine-Machine Interface Access Isolation Policy to No.
  5. Click Save.
