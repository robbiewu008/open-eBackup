Modifying Expiration Warning Days

This section describes how to modify the expiration warning days of a certificate on the product.

Procedure

  1. Choose System > Security > Certificate.
  2. Click More on the right of a certificate and choose Modify Alarm Threshold.
  3. Set the remaining days for alarm reporting. When the remaining certificate valid time reaches the specified value, an alarm is reported.
  4. Click OK.
