Managing Ransomware Protection and WORM Policies

You can view, modify, and delete ransomware protection and WORM policies.

Management Operations

Choose Explore > Protection Policies and click Ransomware Protection and WORM Policies. Table 1 lists related management operations.

Table 1 Ransomware protection and WORM policy management operations

Operation

Description

Navigation Path

Viewing a policy

Scenario

Viewing a policy enables you to know its basic information and associated resources.

In the ransomware protection and WORM policy list, view the policy name, scheduling plan, detection object type, resource type, and description. Creating a Ransomware Protection and WORM Policy describes ransomware protection and WORM policy parameters.

Modifying a policy

Scenario

You can modify the basic information and associated resources of a ransomware protection and WORM policy.

Note

The name of a ransomware protection and WORM policy cannot be changed.

Locate the row that contains the target ransomware protection and WORM policy and choose More > Modify.

Deleting a policy

Scenario

You can delete a ransomware protection and WORM policy that is no longer needed.

Note

A ransomware protection and WORM policy that has been associated with resources cannot be deleted.

Locate the row that contains the target ransomware protection and WORM policy and choose More > Delete.
