Deleting an Air Gap Policy

This section describes how to delete an Air Gap policy that is no longer used.

Precautions

Air Gap policies that have been associated with storage devices cannot be deleted.

Procedure

  1. Choose Data Security > Air Gap.

    For 1.5.0, choose Explore > Air Gap.

  2. Click the Air Gap Policies tab.
  3. Locate the row that contains the item for which the Air Gap policy is to be deleted, and choose More > Delete.
  4. In the dialog box that is displayed, confirm the information and click OK.
