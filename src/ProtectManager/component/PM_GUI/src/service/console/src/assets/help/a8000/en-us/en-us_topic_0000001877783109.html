Stopping a Job

The OceanProtect Appliance allows you to stop backup, replication, and archive jobs.

Procedure

  1. Choose Insight > Jobs.
  2. Click the Current Jobs tab page. The job list is displayed.
  3. Optional: In the upper right corner of the job list, select the start time of the job and SLA policy name to filter jobs. Alternatively, click next to Job Type, Object Type, or Job Status to filter jobs. Or you can click next to Object or Job ID to search for jobs. Or you can click next to Start Time to sort jobs.
  4. Select one or more jobs and click Stop.
  5. In the dialog box that is displayed, click OK.

    After the job is stopped, the job status changes to Stopped, and the job details show that the job is stopped successfully. The following exceptions may occur due to the job time sequence:

    • After you stop a job, the job status changes to Stopped but the details show that the job is successfully executed. The possible cause is that the copy has been generated when you stop the job, that is, the job has been successfully executed. You can view the newly generated copy on the Explore > Copy Data page.
    • After a job is stopped, the job status changes to Failed. The possible cause is that the copy data has been backed up (replicated/archived) when you stop the job, or the job fails when you stop the job. As a result, the job fails to be stopped.
